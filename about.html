Hey, thanks for visiting my site.
I'm Christy Presler.

I specialize in creating custom websites for individuals, small businesses, and non-profits. I enjoy all kinds of design challenges and I'm happy to help you build your brand from the ground up in web and print, or work with you to get your existing brand online to reach a larger audience.

I earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Washington University in St. Louis, where I discovered my love of letterpress, typography, and effective design. This has since grown into my passion for well executed and creative design on the web. I have a passion for helping small businesses succeed and firmly believe that a strong brand identity and strategic web presence is an important part of growing any business. I love using creative critical thinking to tackle tough problems and enable people to pursue their passions.

I'm also an outdoor enthusiast, an Appalachian Trail Thru-Hiker, an avid tea drinker, a book lover, and appreciator of pet rabbits. These days, when not at my computer working on a design or coding a project, I'm often found curled up with a book or working on a woodblock print.

If you have questions, or just want to say hello, feel free to contact me.
